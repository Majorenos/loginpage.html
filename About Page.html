What is LUXMUNDI CUP 2023 all about?


"The Lux Mundis Cup 2023 is the pinnacle of athletic camaraderie and spirited competition, where students come together to showcase their sportmanship and skill in a variety of intramural events. This prestigious tournament not only celebrates the pursuit of physical excellence but also fosters a sense of community and unity among participants, embodying the college's commitment to holistic development and the flourishing of a vibrant, interconnected student body."
